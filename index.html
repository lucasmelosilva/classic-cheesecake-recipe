Classic Cheesecake Recipe

Look no further for a creamy and ultra smooth classic cheesecake recipe! Paired with a buttery graham cracker crust, no one cen deny its simple decadence. For the best results, bake in a water bath.

Ingredients

Graham Cracker Crust
  • 1 and 1/2 cups (150g) graham cracker crumbs (about 10 full sheet graham cracker)
  • 5 Tablespoons (70g) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cups (50g) granulated sugar
Cheesecake
  • four 8-ounce block (904g) full-fat cream-cheese, softened to room temperature
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (240g) sour cream, at room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice (optinal, but recommended)
  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature
  • tooping suggestions: salted caramel, lemon curd, strawberry topping, chocolate ganache, red wine chocolate ganache, fresh fruit, whipped cream. or raspberry sauce (recipe in notes)
